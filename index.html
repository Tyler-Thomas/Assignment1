We need to have a serious conversation about our warming planet

I'm sure you're familiar with the greenhouse effect by now.
Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide, methane and water vapor trap the sun's thermal energy within the atmosphere. This results in a gradual warming of the planet.

Earth's natural regulatory processes already put greenhouse gasses in the atmosphere.
However, human activity is resulting in even more of these gases being poured into the atmosphere.
